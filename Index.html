Money Management Service – Personal Mentor

Our Money Manager helps you track your finances effortlessly. Add amounts with categories like Salary, Transport, Health, Food, and more. Choose Income or Expense, set a date, and add notes for easy reference. View transactions in a clear table, with Income and Expenses shown separately. Each transaction displays a category icon for quick identification. Select a payment method like Cash, Bank Transfer, or Digital Payment for better organization. Stay in control of your spending and savings with our easy-to-use money tracking system! 💰📊💳

Task Management – Personal Mentor

Our Task Manager helps you stay organized. You can add tasks with a title, description, and due date. Sort tasks into categories like Work, Personal, or Urgent. Mark tasks as Completed or Pending and update or delete them anytime. See all your tasks in a clear list to stay focused. Set reminders so you don’t forget important things. Manage your daily work easily and stay on track! ✅📅

Body Monitoring – Personal Mentor

Our BMI Calculator helps you check if your weight is healthy. Just enter your height and weight, and it will quickly calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI). It tells you if you are underweight, normal, overweight, or obese. This helps you understand your fitness level. Use it to track your progress and stay healthy. Simple, fast, and easy to use! 🏋️‍♂️💪
